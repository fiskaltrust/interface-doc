## Purpose

This guide explains how to manually install the fiskaltrust InStore App on Android devices via the Google Play Store.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- An Android device with access to the Google Play Store
- A stable internet connection

## Step 1: Open the Google Play Store

1. On your Android device, locate and tap the **Google Play Store** app icon.

## Step 2: Search for the fiskaltrust InStore App

1. Tap the **Search** bar at the top of the screen.
2. Enter: `fiskaltrust InStore App`

Alternatively, you can directly open the app page using this link:
- [fiskaltrust InStore App on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.fiskaltrust.instore.app)

## Step 3: Install the App

1. Locate the **fiskaltrust InStore App** in the search results.
2. Tap on the app to open its detail page.
3. Tap the **Install** button.
4. Wait for the download and installation to complete.

:::tip

Ensure your device has sufficient storage space before installing the app.

:::

## Step 4: Open the App

1. Once the installation is complete, tap **Open** directly from the Google Play Store.
2. Alternatively, locate the **fiskaltrust InStore App** icon on your home screen or app drawer and tap to open.

## Next Steps

After installation, continue with starting and configuring the app in our [setup guide](../../Setup-guide/setup.md).

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during installation:

- **App not found**: Ensure your device is compatible and your region supports the app.
- **Installation fails**: Check your internet connection and available storage space.
- **Google Play Store not available**: Consider using the [manual download](../manual/manual-guide.md) method instead.
